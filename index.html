Bass-ic Records is Ireland's newest fake music label representating some of the country's best talent.


What is Bass-ic Records?

Bass-ic Records is a fictional music label devised for a college project. (Nothing on this website is true!) With a fictional roster that includes some of the globes finest talent across all genres, Bass-ic Records is unbeatable. Founded in 2021 by Daniel, Colum & Ciara – all avid music fans.

Some of the artists on our fictional label include Rise Against, Green Day, Metallica, Taylor Swift, Pink, Justin Bieber, Rihanna, Beyonce, Drake, Johnny Cash, John Denver and Steve Earle, amongst many others.

How to use this website?

Want to get to know our signed artists a little better? Go to our ‘Recommendations’ page to search our roster either by Genre or Similar Artist.

Want to see any of our artists past performances? Head over to our ‘Gigs’ page to search your favourite artist and where they previously performed on recent tours.

If you are a new artist interested in sending us your demo, or just have any questions for our team please fill in your details on our “Contact Us” page and a member of the team will come back to you.
